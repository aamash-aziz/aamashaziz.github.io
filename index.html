About the School

Aziz Public Model School is the flagship institution of the Aziz Group of Schools. It is a premier co-educational school offering education from Nursery through Matriculation. The school is committed to providing a nurturing, stimulating, and challenging academic environment that fosters intellectual curiosity and personal growth in every student.

Aziz Kids

🌿 Early Childhood👶 Age 2–6 Years📍 Multan

About Aziz Kids

Aziz Kids is a dedicated early childhood education center providing a warm, loving, and stimulating environment for children aged 2 to 6 years. Our play-based and inquiry-driven curriculum develops cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills during the most critical years of a child's development.

Programs Offered

We offer Toddler Program (age 2-3), Nursery (age 3-4), Kindergarten I and II, with age-appropriate activities, storytelling, music, art, and early literacy and numeracy.

Features & Activities

🎠 Indoor & Outdoor Play Area
📚 Storytelling & Reading Circles
🎨 Arts, Crafts & Music
🧩 Learning Through Play
🍎 Nutrition & Health Program
